BJJ Training Guide
Age: 13   |   Height: 175 cm   |   Weight: 55 kg
BJJ Days: Mon / Wed / Sat
Goal: Build strength, coordination, endurance & prevent injury.

Monday — BJJ

  • Technique + Movement Drills
  • Light post-session stretch (hips & shoulders)

Tuesday — Strength & Coordination

Warm-up: Skipping rope 2 min, bear & crab crawls 20 m each, hip openers

  • Bodyweight Squats × 15
  • Push-ups × 10
  • Plank Shoulder Taps × 20
  • Glute Bridge × 15
  • Side Plank Hold × 20 sec each side

Rest 60 sec between rounds (3 rounds total)

Optional: 3 × 20 m sprints or 3 × 30 sec bike

Wednesday — BJJ

  • Rolling & Drills
  • Gentle foam roll before bed

Thursday — Strength & Agility

Warm-up: Skips / hops / lunges / band pull-aparts

  • Jump Squats × 10
  • Inverted Rows (TRX or table) × 8
  • Push-ups × 10
  • Bird-Dogs × 12 each side
  • Kettlebell Swings × 10

Rest 60 sec between rounds (3–4 rounds)

Agility: Sprawls × 20

Friday — Mobility / Recovery

  • 20–30 min: Foam roll, pigeon stretch, shoulder dislocates, deep squat hold
  • Breathing drills: 5 min slow nasal breathing

Saturday — BJJ

  • Sparring & Situational Drills
  • Ice or cold shower after training

Sunday — Active Play / Light Cardio

  • Skate, surf, swim, cycle, or jog 20–30 min
  • Purpose: build aerobic base & keep training fun

Tips

  • Focus on technique & consistency, not weight
  • Keep sessions short (30–45 min) and fun
  • Drink water throughout the day
  • Eat protein, carbs & veggies for recovery
  • Sleep 8–9 hrs nightly for best performance
